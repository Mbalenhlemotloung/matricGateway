About Us

Matric Gateway is a student developed platform created to support learners in their academic journey. The platform was designed to simplify access to study materials, improve understanding of key subjects and provide learners with a structured way to request academic assistance.

We understand the challenges students face when preparing for examinations, including lack of resources, unclear explanations and difficulty staying consistent with studying. This platform aims to address those challenges by offering organized, accessible and easy to use support tools.
